About

George Papadopoulos was born in Veria in 2001. He graduated from the 3rd General High School of Veria in 2019, where he continued his studies at the Department of Computer Engineering, Computers and Telecommunications of the International Hellenic University. At the present time he is in the 2nd year of his undergraduate studies in the university curriculum of the department. In parallel with his studies, he deals in more detail with more specific issues in his field, such as the development of applications for smartphones, the development of the backend of websites as well as the development of software. In his free time he likes to create. That was the main reason he decided to start participate in European School Radio. Since April 2019 he is a member of the scientific company "Cross-thematic, Cross-Cultural Radio of the Educational Community with distinctive title European School Radio", where he deals mainly with the technical part.
